Homemade pizza recipie

  1. Gather all your ingredients, you'll need:
    • dough
    • tomato sauce
    • cheese
    • your choice of toppings
  2. spread the dough into a big circle, then add your tomato sauce
  3. add the cheese, then add your toppings
  4. cook in the oven until pizza is cooked
  5. lets the pizza cool and then enjoy!
